Achernar

Nomenclature
This star bears the traditional name of Achernar (sometimes spelled Achenar), derived from the Arabic آخر النهر ākhir an-nahr, meaning "The End of the River". This name originally referred to Theta Eridani instead, which latterly was known by the similar traditional name Acamar, with the same etymology.
